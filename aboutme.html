About Me

Hello, my name is Giovani Giagnacovo, I am an Electrical Engineering major at Texas A&M University interested in hardware design and its applications. I am currently a member of IEEE, and the Aggie Coding Club at Texas A&M University. I am also involved in the TAMU Robomasters electrical design team and the 3D CAD labratory at Texas A&M University.


I have experience in PCB Design, FPGA system design, and programming microcontrollers. I am proficient in Verilog, VHDL, SystemVerilog, C++, and Python. Additionally, I have experience using various software, including: Altium Designer, Quartus Prime, Vivado, ModelSim, and Fusion360.


My goal is to secure an internship in Electrical design for the summer of 2025. I hope to secure an internship where I can contribute to exciting projects and develop my skills. I aim to one day specialize in Electrical design and development of Electrical systems. My goal is to one day make a real-world impact contributing to a project that will push the boundaries of technology.
